## Summary
Nuwellis (Nasdaq: NUWE)has receivedFDA 510(k) clearancefor a new size of its Dual Lumen Extended Length Catheter (dELC). The catheter, available in11 cm and 15 cm lengths, is designed for peripheral venous access to support ultrafiltration therapy with the Aquadex system for up to 72 hours.The clearance advances Nuwellis' strategy to build a comprehensive fluid management platform across various care settings, from ICU to outpatient facilities. The dELC features coil reinforcement and dual-lumen design for consistent blood flow, supporting the company's focus onthree growth areas: critical care, cardiac surgery recovery, and hospital-based outpatient heart failure programs.
