## Summary
The Children's Place (NASDAQ:PLCE)has announced a multi-season collaboration withSanrio®, launching a series of limited-edition capsule collections featuring Hello Kitty and Friends from Fall 2025 through Spring 2026 and beyond.The collaboration includes four themed collections:Perfect Paris(Fall 2025),Trick or Treat with Hello Kitty and Friends(Halloween 2025),Forever Fancy(Holiday 2025), andGalentine's with Hello Kitty and Friends®(Spring 2026). The first full capsule collection, Perfect Paris, will launch onSeptember 11th, 2025.This partnership aims to combine Sanrio's iconic characters with The Children's Place's signature quality and value, creating collectible and wearable fashion that appeals to both millennial parents and their children.
